About Me

Hello! I am Dr. Bornali Phukon, currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the Beckman Institute, University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I work as part of the Statistical Speech Technology group on the Speech Accessibility Project. My research focuses on improving voice recognition technology for diverse speech patterns and disabilities.

Research Interests

My work spans the areas of Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR), semantic metrics, large language models (LLMs), and evaluation of ASR systems for dysarthric speech. I am particularly interested in developing tools and metrics to assess ASR models for diverse speech patterns, as well as integrating ASR systems with error correction models.
